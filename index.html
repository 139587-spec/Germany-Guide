Explore the Diverse Cultures of Germany

Germany is a very diverse country with many cultures and various different food varieties. Germany is culturally and economically rich. It is most redominantly known for its Berlin capital and many tourist attractions within it. Although it is one country, it is technically grouped into 4 part, North, South, West, and East. Each section has cultural connectivity, but are unique in many factors, such as food and tourist attractions!
