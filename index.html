Explore the Diverse Cultures of Germany!

Germany is a very diverse country with many cultures and various different food varieties. Germany is culturally and economically rich. It is most redominantly known for its Berlin capital and many tourist attractions within it. Although it is one country, it is technically grouped into 4 parts, North, South, West, and East. Each section has cultural connectivity, but are unique in many factors, such as food and tourist attractions! Although there have been problems in the past with land, Germany has shown a country can survive the harsh times and become one of a kind! The country itself shows perserverance and shows people how countries can overcome their differences and live in not only harmony, but excel in their fields and create wonderful architecture, foods, and culture that has not only brought the country together, but millions of tourists from other countries as well! I urge you to look through this website to further learn about the culture of Germany, and if you are going to travel there, you can also learn lots of helpful tips that will aid your travel!
